🗓️ UAE Seasonality & Special Events

💡 Why Seasonality Matters

Shopping behavior in UAE changes dramatically during key periods. Plan your ad spend, inventory, and content around these high-traffic times for maximum ROI.

Month 🎉 Event 📊 Traffic 💰 Ad Spend 📝 Action Items
January New Year Sales 🟡 Medium Normal New year organization resolutions content
February - 🟢 Low Reduce Build content, prep for Ramadan
March* 🌙 Ramadan Start 🔴 HIGH +50-100% Ramadan deals, home prep content, gifting
April* 🎁 Eid Al-Fitr 🔴 HIGH +50-100% Eid gifts, last-minute deals, free shipping
May - 🟢 Low Reduce Summer prep, focus on organic
June* 🐑 Eid Al-Adha 🟡 Medium +25% Eid promotions, family gifting
July ☀️ Summer Sales 🟢 Low Reduce Summer clearance, build email list
August 🎒 Back to School 🔴 HIGH +50-75% Desk organization, study setup content
September 🏫 School Starts 🟡 Medium-High +25% Home office for parents, student deals
October - 🟡 Medium Normal Prep White Friday, build hype
November 🛍️ WHITE FRIDAY 🔴 PEAK +100-150% Biggest discounts, aggressive ads, stock up
December 🎄 Year-End Sales 🟡 Medium-High +25-50% Holiday gifts, year-end clearance

* Islamic holiday dates shift yearly. Check exact dates for current year.

🌙 Ramadan (30 days)

  • Shopping peaks after Iftar (8-11 PM)
  • Home organization for gatherings
  • Gifting culture - bundle deals work
  • Last 10 days = Eid prep rush
  • Action: Start ads 2 weeks before

🎒 Back to School (Aug-Sep)

  • Parents buying desk organization
  • Student study setup content
  • Target: Dubai, Abu Dhabi schools
  • Peak: Last 2 weeks of August
  • Action: Start content mid-July

🛍️ White Friday (Nov 20-30)

  • UAE's Black Friday equivalent
  • Biggest shopping week of the year
  • Customers expect 20-50% off
  • Ad costs spike - book early
  • Action: Stock up October, ads Nov 1

✅ Seasonal Prep Checklist

Mark key dates in Google Calendar
Set ad budget reminders (2 weeks before)
Stock up inventory before peak seasons
Prepare seasonal content/graphics
Plan discount codes for each event
Review competitor pricing before sales

🔄 How to Automate SWOT Review

📅 Schedule Monthly Review

  1. Set calendar reminder: 1st of each month
  2. Review each quadrant (15 mins)
  3. Update checkboxes based on progress
  4. Add new items discovered
  5. Create 3 action items for the month

📊 Data Sources for SWOT

  • Strengths: Customer feedback, sales data
  • Weaknesses: Returns, complaints, cart abandonment
  • Opportunities: Google Alerts, trends, competitors
  • Threats: News, competitor prices, market changes

✅ Action Item Rules

  • Max 3 actions per category per month
  • Each action must be specific & measurable
  • Set deadline for each action
  • Mark complete or carry forward
  • Celebrate wins! 🎉
