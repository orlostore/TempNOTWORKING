ORLO Store متجر أورلو

ORLO Store is a Dubai-based online shop offering a carefully curated mix of unique home décor, handmade gifts, and practical everyday essentials — all delivered across the UAE.

Our collection includes handcrafted fridge magnets, miniature vintage figurines (rotary phones, radios, gramophones, TVs), motivational wall hangings, and cable management solutions — something for every home and every occasion.

We ship across all Emirates with free delivery on orders over AED 75. Bilingual support in English and Arabic via WhatsApp.

Why Shop at ORLO?

• Unique handmade and decorative items you won't find elsewhere

• Bilingual service in English and Arabic
