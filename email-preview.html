Email templates · all 12, live

Every transactional email the site sends, rendered from the production functions/api/email-template.js. This is what customers receive today.

Stripe receipts are NOT in this preview. Stripe sends its own receipt + invoice PDFs directly (the dark-navy "Receipt from ORLO Store" email). Customisable only via Stripe Dashboard (logo + brand color). Separate from these templates.

One template, every email

All customer emails use the same customerEmail() shell — the difference between them is just the copy passed in (titleEn, bodyEn, bodyAr, infoBoxEn, ctaUrl/ctaText). Same for the 2 admin emails using adminEmail().

The footer block at the bottom of every email is the canonical _footer-canonical-email.html, synced into both this mockup and the production template by scripts/sync-email-footer.js. Drift between the two is blocked at commit-time.
