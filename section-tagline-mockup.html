Notes on this register

  • Layout: centered, max-width ~680px, ~56-64px vertical padding above and below. Sits as its OWN section above the product strip — not crammed inside it.
  • Top meta line: "Popular Now" / "Shop by Category" / "New Arrivals" in DM Sans caps, navy at 60%. Acts as the orientation label so a passive scroller still knows which section they're in.
  • Title: Cormorant Garamond italic 300, ~2-2.8rem (responsive). Coral accent on one phrase per title (the em) — same trick The Edit teaser uses.
  • Description: Cormorant Garamond italic 400, ~1-1.2rem, max-width 520px to force a 2-3 line wrap. Always 2-3 lines, never 1, never 4+.
  • CTA: DM Sans 500, caps, letter-spaced 0.24em, navy underline. Optional — for sections where the section IS the CTA (the products themselves), you can omit it.
  • No Arabic in the editorial block. The Arabic still appears in the actual product cards below (names, prices, CTAs) — but the editorial voice is English-only, matching Hermès on bilingual surfaces.
  • Coral budget: one coral italic em per title. Three total on the page (one per section) — within the editorial budget, not loud.
  • Consistency with The Edit: exact same component as .edit-teaser-v2 (meta + h2 + p + cta). One pattern, used four times across the page.
