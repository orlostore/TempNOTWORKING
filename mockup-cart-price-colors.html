Design Notes

  • "Add to Cart" → Orange: Warm orange is a classic CTA color — draws the eye and invites action. Currently the button blends with the nav (both blue).
  • Prices → Blue: Shifts prices from "sale" energy (orange) to a premium, trustworthy feel. Blue reads as confident & established.
  • Visual hierarchy: Orange is now reserved for action elements only (buttons, badges). Blue handles information (prices, nav). Clear separation of "look" vs "act".
  • Cart icon untouched: Nav bar cart stays as-is (white icon, orange count badge).
  • Scope: Index page only. Product page buybox is a separate discussion.
