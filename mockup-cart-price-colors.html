Design Notes

  • Cart → Orange: Makes the cart icon pop on the dark blue nav bar. Draws the eye toward the shopping action — a subtle CTA.
  • Prices → Blue: Shifts prices from "sale" energy (orange) to a premium, trustworthy feel. Blue prices read as confident & established.
  • Badge stays orange: The little cart-count dot is already orange — now the icon matches it for a unified look.
  • Orange is reserved for CTAs: With prices in blue, orange becomes exclusively for action elements (buttons, badges, cart) — cleaner visual hierarchy.
  • Scope: This only affects the index page. Product page buybox prices are a separate discussion.
