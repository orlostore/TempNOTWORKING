The Corgi sits low and bright on your dashboard — ears up, chest forward, with the slightly imperious composure only a corgi knows how to carry.

Below the head, a brass-weighted spring. Every speed bump, every red-light stop, every gentle accel out of a parking lot — the corgi nods with you. Restrained enthusiasm. Patient company.

For the commute that needs a little softening. For the dashboard that has been too empty for too long.

Specification
  • Hand-painted resin, ~9.5 cm tall
  • Brass-weighted spring neck — smooth, slow recoil
  • Free-standing, holds steady at cruising speed
  • Hand-finished in small batches
